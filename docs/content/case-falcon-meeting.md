# Case: The Falcon Meeting

## Premise
An informant claims to be waiting at a cafe for a meeting with a local official. Metadata places the informant's phone near the airport instead.

## Cast
- **Falcon** — informant
- **Local Official** — suspected contact
- **HIA Handler** — suspected rival intermediary

## Locations
- Cafe
- Airport perimeter
- Official residence

## Evidence plan
### Inbox claim
"I'm at the cafe. Official is late. Need guidance."

### SIGINT clue
Phone ping near airport perimeter shortly before or around the email.

### Intercept clue
Ambiguous conversation suggesting a shipment, handoff, or route change.

### Nominals clue
Official has prior indirect ties to airport-linked logistics or shell-company activity.

## Hidden truth
Falcon is compromised. The cafe report is bait. The real handoff is happening near the airport.
