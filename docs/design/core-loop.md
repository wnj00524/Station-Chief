# Core Gameplay Loop

1. New information arrives.
2. The player opens multiple apps to compare claims against records and metadata.
3. The player may assign a staff task for verification.
4. Time continues advancing during investigation.
5. The player commits to an intervention or report.
6. The world responds.
7. Political Capital and future information access reflect the outcome.

## Design requirement
Winning should come from interpretation quality, not from noticing highlighted UI prompts.
