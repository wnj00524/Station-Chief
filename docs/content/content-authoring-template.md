# Content Authoring Template

Use this structure for future cases:

## Premise
What appears to be happening.

## Hidden truth
What is actually happening.

## Misleading evidence
What could lead the player toward the wrong conclusion.

## Confirming evidence
What supports the correct hypothesis.

## Time windows
When evidence arrives and when the opportunity expires.

## Player actions
What interventions are allowed.

## Outcomes
Success, partial success, failure, and defensive outcomes.
